Fries

Description

Some delicious grill fries!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Fry oil
  • Sauces
  • Vinegar

Steps:

  1. Cut the potatoes with the cutting tool
  2. Let the potatoes rest into fresh water with vinegar
  3. Dry the potatoes after 15 minutes
  4. Prepare the oil
  5. Fry the potatoes
  6. After they're cooked and crunchy, serve them on a plate with your favorite sauces
  7. Eat them!
