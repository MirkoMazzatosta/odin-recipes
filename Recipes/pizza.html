Pizza

Description

A piping hot pizza!

Ingredients:

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Some basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Pizza dough: it's not that easy!
  2. Let it rest for some hours
  3. Once the dough is ready, shape it like a disc
  4. Once you gave it the shape, pour some tomato sauce on its top
  5. Add some Mozzarella slices
  6. Decorate it with some basil leaves
  7. Enjoy your soft pizza!
