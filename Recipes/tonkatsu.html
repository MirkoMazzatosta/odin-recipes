Tonkatsu

Description

A well cooked Tonkatsu!

Ingredients

  • Panko
  • Pork chops
  • Eggs
  • Fry oil
  • Greens
  • Lemon
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Dip the pork chops into the eggs yolk
  2. Dip the pork chops into the panko
  3. Fry the pork chops
  4. After they're fried, serve them on a plate with some side greens, lemon and sauces!
